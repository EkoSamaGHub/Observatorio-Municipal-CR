# 13. Suggested Commercial Branding

Branding must read as **institutional, trustworthy, and government-appropriate** — closer to civic
infrastructure than to a startup app. It should work in Spanish-first markets and scale beyond
embassies to the broader public sector.

## 13.1 Naming strategy

Two layers: a **customer-facing product brand** (what an embassy buys) and a **white-label platform
brand** (the engine resellers/partners deploy).

### Product name candidates (citizen-attention platform)

| Name | Read | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **ConsulAI** | "Consul" + AI | Clear consular fit; may feel narrow when expanding beyond embassies. |
| **Cónsul** / **Cónsul Digital** | "Digital consul" | Spanish-native, dignified, official. Strong for embassy market. |
| **Atiende** | "It attends/serves" (ES verb) | Citizen-attention essence; warm, action-oriented, public-sector-friendly. |
| **Ventanilla** / **Ventanilla AI** | "Service window" (ES) | Instantly legible to LATAM citizens — the official window, now digital. |
| **Asesor Oficial** | "Official adviser" | Conveys authority + grounding. |
| **Enlace** | "Link / liaison" | Neutral, scales across institutions. |

> **Recommended product brand:** **Cónsul** (or **ConsulAI** for English-leaning/tech audiences) for
> the embassy market; **Ventanilla** as the broader public-sector citizen-attention brand when
> expanding to municipalities/agencies.

### White-label / platform-engine name candidates

| Name | Read | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **GovGrounded** | "Government + grounded answers" | Encodes the core promise (grounded, controlled). Great for B2B/partner positioning. |
| **Fundamenta** | "It grounds/founds" (ES) | Elegant Spanish double meaning: grounded answers + solid foundation. |
| **Andamio** | "Scaffold" (ES) | The infrastructure others build on. |
| **CivicGrounded / Civica** | civic + grounded | Public-sector breadth. |

> **Recommended platform brand:** **GovGrounded** (engine/partner-facing) — the trust promise is in
> the name. Pair with the localized product brand for end clients.

## 13.2 Positioning ideas

- **Category creation:** don't compete in "chatbots." Define the category: *"Institutional
  Citizen-Attention Platform."* Own the trust axis.
- **Taglines**
  - *"Información oficial, al instante."* (Official information, instantly.)
  - *"La ventanilla oficial, ahora 24/7."* (The official window, now 24/7.)
  - *"AI que solo responde con fuentes oficiales."* (AI that answers only with official sources.)
  - English (partner/investor): *"Grounded AI for government. Answers it can stand behind."*
- **Message hierarchy:** Trust → Control → Official sources → Always-available → Insightful. Lead with
  trust in every gov conversation; lead with ROI/insight with operations buyers.

## 13.3 Government-friendly brand attributes

| Attribute | Expression |
|---|---|
| **Dignified** | Restrained typography, official tone, minimal/no playful illustration. |
| **Trustworthy** | Calm palette (deep blues/teals, neutral grays), clear source citations as a visible brand element. |
| **Neutral** | Avoid partisan/national-political symbolism in the *platform* brand; let each institution's own seal/branding lead in their deployment. |
| **Accessible** | High-contrast, WCAG AA, plain-language copy, Spanish-first. |
| **Sovereign-ready** | Visual + messaging emphasis on data control, residency, auditability. |

Each deployment foregrounds the **client institution's own identity** (e.g., the embassy's seal and
name) — the platform brand stays in a discreet "powered by" position. Governments want *their* face on
the citizen channel.

## 13.4 White-label strategy

- **Theming**: per-tenant logo, colors, name, custom domain for the dashboard; citizen-facing
  identity is fully the institution's (WhatsApp display name, profile, welcome).
- **Reseller program**: integrators/agencies in other countries deploy under **GovGrounded** as the
  engine with their own front brand; platform fee + per-deployment license (§8/§9).
- **Brand architecture**:
  - *GovGrounded* — the platform/engine (B2B, partners, procurement, investors).
  - *Cónsul / Ventanilla / Atiende* — end-client product brands by vertical (embassies / municipal /
    general public sector).
  - *[Institution] Atención al Ciudadano* — what the citizen sees in WhatsApp.
- **Trademark/domain check** required before committing — validate availability in CR/CO and target
  markets; the names above are candidates, not cleared marks.

## 13.5 Recommended brand stack (starting point)

> **Engine:** GovGrounded · **Embassy product:** Cónsul (ConsulAI in EN) · **Public-sector product:**
> Ventanilla · **Promise:** "Official information, instantly — answers backed by official sources." ·
> **Palette:** deep institutional blue/teal + neutral gray · **Hero feature in all branding:** the
> visible source citation, because the citation *is* the differentiator.
